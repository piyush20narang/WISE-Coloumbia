Spreading the Light of Spiritual Wisdom

WISE Columbia, Inc. is a Missouri 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization inspired by the teachings of His Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, Founder Acarya of International Society of Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON).

Our purpose is to spread the awareness and importance of value-based spiritual education, helping individuals discover their true identity and purpose through Vedic knowledge.

  • Spread awareness and importance of value-based Spiritual Education
  • Develop holistic and sustainable farming projects including Horticulture and Dairy
  • Build cooperation with sister organizations ISKCON St. Louis and Rupanuga Vedic College, Kansas City
  • Provide spiritual education support to other non-profit organizations in our community

Srila Prabhupada paraphrased the British Poet Cowper,
“The city is made by man and the country is made by God.”

So we call God as Krishna — this organization will be administered by Krishna and we will serve Krishna as His servitors.
