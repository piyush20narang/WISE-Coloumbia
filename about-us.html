Our History

Wisdom in Spiritual Education (W.I.S.E.) Columbia, Inc. was born out of a desire to provide a structured platform for spiritual learning in the heart of Missouri. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to sharing the timeless wisdom of the Vedas with a modern audience.

What started as small home gatherings for Bhagavad Gita study has grown into a community organization that hosts festivals, youth programs, and educational seminars. We work closely with our sister organizations, ISKCON St. Louis and Rupanuga Vedic College in Kansas City, to bring world-class spiritual teachers to Columbia.
