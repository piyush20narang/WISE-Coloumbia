Past Highlights

Kids Rath Yatra in June 2025

Children gathered with enthusiasm at the Cart Festival to honor Lord Jagannath, the Lord of the Universe, who is the father and mother of all living beings. Parade in the neighbourhood streets with cheerful singing of Harinam Sankirtan filled the crisp summer evening with joy and love. As taught by Krisna Das Kaviraj Goswami, author of Caitanya Caritamrita:

“Anyone who hears about the description of the car festival of Lord Ship of Jagannath, Baldev and Subhadra Maharani will attain Śrī Caitanya Mahaprabhu.”

He will also attain the elevated state with which he will have firm conviction in devotional service and love of God.

  • No. of participation-25-30
  • The neighbourhood selected for the Rath Yatra was in Steeplechase/Thornbrook neighbourhood
  • It was well received by the young families. Children and Families from diverse communities participated in the parade with great joy and honor.
  • A sumptuous Vegetarian Feast (Prasadam) was served.
