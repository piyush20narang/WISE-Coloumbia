




Short Biography of Lal Gopal das

Sri Lal Gopal das is a spiritual leader who delivers discourses on Vedic scriptures, such as the Bhagavad Gita, Srimad Bhagavatam, Vishnu Puranam, Ramayana, Mahabharata, and Vedanta texts. Lal Gopal Das is a lifelong student and guide of India’s sacred Vedic tradition, illuminating timeless truths for modern seekers.

For the past two decades he has lectured as a service to many organizations in the St Louis area, such as ISKCON of St. Louis and its Goloka Children’s School, Balvihar of St Louis, The Hindu Temple of St. Louis, Missouri Ganesha Temple, Durga Temple and BAPS. He has been invited as a special speaker to lecture at educational organizations, such as the Doctor’s Association, Washington University and St. Louis University. He has also extended his discourses to regions outside of the Missouri area such as, New Jersey, Pennsylvania , Vancouver, etc.

For the past five years, up until May 2024, Lal Gopal das served as the President of ISKCON of St. Louis. He has now taken up the role as the Zonal Supervisor, overseeing and providing guidance to multiple ISKCON centers in Missouri and a few of the centers in Illinois.  

He also manages his YouTube channel “Daily Gita Bytes” where he regularly posts videos. To date, he has delivered over 1,000 discourses. Currently, he is conducting a course titled, “Flavors of Vaishnavism,” via his YouTube channel.

Lal Gopal das was initiated into the Gaudiya Vaishnava lineage by his Guru, His Holiness Radhanath Swami, who is a direct disciple of His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, Founder-Acarya of the International Society for Krishna Consciousness.

Lal Gopal das holds a Master’s Degree in Informational Technology from Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology, in Melbourne, Australia. He currently works as a Director for a Telecom company specializing in cybersecurity.
